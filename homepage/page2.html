After high school I started studying civil engineering in Arnhem, after a few months it became apparent that the study was not suited for me so I quit. After half a year of working I started studying Life sciences at the HAN university of applied sciences in Nijmegen. The first two years of the study have been focussed on general laboratory techniques and science. The first half of my third year I specialised in molecular plant biology and in this second half I follow a CS50 course as minor. In the picture I am holding a pipetboy.
